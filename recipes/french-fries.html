French Fries Recipe Page

Description:

These homemade French fries are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. Perfectly seasoned and easy to make, they are a great side dish or snack.

Recipes:

  • Potatoes: Use Russet potatoes for the best texture.
  • Oil: Vegetable or canola oil works well for frying.
  • Salt: Season to taste after frying.
  • Optional spices: Add paprika, garlic powder, or pepper for extra flavor.

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Drain and pat the potatoes dry with a paper towel.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Season with salt and serve hot.
